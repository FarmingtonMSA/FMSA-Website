Events

Join Us at Our Upcoming Performances

FMSA hosts a variety of events throughout the year, from intimate concerts in retirement homes to larger scale collaborations with charities and foundations. We also support and promote young artist recitals.

All official event programs, schedules, and volunteer sign-ups are managed through our community Discord server.

Membership

We welcome all students that have a passion for music and service. Non-musicians can always join to assist with fundraising and internal activities. Musicians can join with a school club or by registering independently.

To get started, please reach out to us via our contact information or our social media channels.
