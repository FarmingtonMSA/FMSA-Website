Our Global Network

We are proud to partner with a diverse range of local and international organizations. These collaborations offer our members meaningful opportunities for artistic growth, mentorship, and global connection through music.

Probus Greater Hartford

Probus is a vital community group proudly serving over 400 individuals with disabilities. We collaborate with them to be adaptive and introduce rural music to their members. Our partnership focuses on creating joyful musical experiences to foster connection through music.

Lower Mainland Music Students' Association

Vancouver, Canada

We have a close relationship with our founding peers at the LMMSA in Vancouver. This connection allows us to have cross-border feedback with each other, support fundraisers, and culturally exchange ideas. We are proud to be a part of this international student-led music assembly.
