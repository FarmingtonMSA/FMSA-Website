Academic Excellence

Exploring the Science of Music

Scholars in Sound is our academic division, exploring the science behind the therapeutic effects of music. We regularly publish student-written articles, curate performance videos, and create calming playlists.

AI-Generated Compositions

This interdisciplinary library of music, featuring student-coded AI-generated compositions, serves as a soothing resource for patients, connecting our passion for music with new innovations.

Global Connections

We foster cross-cultural communication through connections with student groups in Canada, China, and Australia. Locally, we partner with art galleries to create events that blend visual and auditory art.

Digital Bridge

Our student-coded AI helps elders connect with the increasingly fast-paced and digital world through music, bridging generational gaps with technology.
