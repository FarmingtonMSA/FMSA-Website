Welcome to FMSA

Farmington Music Students Association

A student-led nonprofit that believes in the healing power of music. We bring people together through shared sound and emotion, using music as a bridge between generations, cultures, and hearts.

Learn More Join Us

Our Mission

Our organization is built on two branches. Community Outreach brings live performances and music therapy to local spaces such as senior centers, libraries, and schools. Scholars in Sound extends our reach globally, curating performances, AI-generated compositions, and playlists for hospitals and care centers around the world.

Every note we play carries the same purpose: to bring comfort, joy, and connection through the language of music.

Our Impact

Community Outreach

Our Community Outreach branch brings the restorative power of music directly to those who need it most. Through live performances and music therapy programs, we visit senior living communities, retirement homes, and centers for individuals with disabilities, using music to spark joy and connection.

This initiative has grown to include hospitals, where our performances and curated sound experiences offer comfort to patients and their families around the world.

500+ Performances

Academic Excellence

Scholars in Sound

Scholars in Sound is our academic division, exploring the science behind the therapeutic effects of music. We regularly publish student-written articles, curate performance videos, and create calming playlists.

AI-Generated Compositions

This interdisciplinary library of music, featuring student-coded AI-generated compositions, serves as a soothing resource for patients, connecting our passion for music with new innovations.

Global Connections

We foster cross-cultural communication through connections with student groups in Canada, China, and Australia. Locally, we partner with art galleries to create events that blend visual and auditory art.

Digital Bridge

Our student-coded AI helps elders connect with the increasingly fast-paced and digital world through music, bridging generational gaps with technology.

Why Join FMSA?

Empowerment and Support

We strive to make every member feel valued and heard in a community where they are supported. Your voice drives our organization, whether you're suggesting a performance venue, looking for advice from a music instructor, or contributing ideas for one of our academic projects.

Our Global Network

We are proud to partner with a diverse range of local and international organizations. These collaborations offer our members meaningful opportunities for artistic growth, mentorship, and global connection through music.

Probus Greater Hartford

Probus is a vital community group proudly serving over 400 individuals with disabilities. We collaborate with them to be adaptive and introduce rural music to their members. Our partnership focuses on creating joyful musical experiences to foster connection through music.

Lower Mainland Music Students' Association

Vancouver, Canada

We have a close relationship with our founding peers at the LMMSA in Vancouver. This connection allows us to have cross-border feedback with each other, support fundraisers, and culturally exchange ideas. We are proud to be a part of this international student-led music assembly.
